WWE

WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) is the world’s largest professional wrestling and sports-entertainment company. It combines athletic wrestling matches with scripted storylines, characters, and dramatic rivalries, similar to a live-action TV drama with sports elements. This was found in 1953 in Connectiut.

There are 4 main shows: Monday Night RAW, Friday Night Smackdown and WWE NXT and the new one, Evolve
